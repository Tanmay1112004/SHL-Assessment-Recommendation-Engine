How It Works

1

Natural Language Processing

Our AI analyzes job descriptions using advanced NLP techniques to extract key skills, requirements, and competencies.

2

Semantic Analysis

We use semantic similarity algorithms to match extracted skills with our comprehensive database of SHL assessments.

3

Intelligent Scoring

Each assessment receives a confidence score based on multiple factors including skill overlap, relevance, and context.

4

Optimized Recommendations

Results are ranked and presented with detailed explanations to help you make informed hiring decisions.
